FAQs About Gynecomastia Surgery in Jaipur

As you consider gynecomastia surgery in Jaipur, it’s natural to have questions. Here are answers to some commonly asked questions to help you make an informed decision:

1. What is the recovery period after gynecomastia surgery?

After gynecomastia surgery, you can expect a recovery period of approximately 1 to 2 weeks. During this time, it’s important to avoid strenuous activities that could hinder the healing process. Following our post-operative instructions diligently will contribute to a smoother and faster recovery.

2. Will there be visible scars?

Our skilled surgeons employ precise techniques to minimize scarring. Incisions are strategically placed to ensure discreet and inconspicuous scars over time. While some initial scarring may occur, they tend to fade significantly as you continue to heal.

3. Is gynecomastia surgery permanent?

Yes, gynecomastia surgery provides permanent results. The excess breast tissue and fat that are removed during the surgery do not typically grow back. However, maintaining a healthy lifestyle that includes regular exercise and a balanced diet can help sustain the outcomes achieved.

4. Are the results immediate?

You will notice a significant improvement immediately after gynecomastia surgery. However, some initial swelling and bruising are normal. As the swelling subsides over the following weeks, the final results become more apparent. It’s important to be patient during the healing process.

5. Are there any potential risks?

Like any surgical procedure, gynecomastia surgery carries certain risks. These may include infection, bleeding, scarring, and temporary changes in sensation around the treated area. However, our team takes every precaution to minimize these risks and provide a safe surgical experience.
