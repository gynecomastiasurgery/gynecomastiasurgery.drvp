Gynecomastia surgery is typically performed once the patient’s chest is fully developed. This usually occurs after the age of 18. It’s important to ensure that your body has reached a stable state before considering surgery for optimal results.

During the initial recovery period, some discomfort is to be expected. However, any discomfort can be managed with the prescribed pain medications. As your body heals, the discomfort will gradually subside, and you’ll start feeling more comfortable.

Efforts are made to minimize scarring through strategic incision placement. While some scars are inevitable, they tend to fade over time and can be discreetly hidden by clothing. Our surgical techniques are designed to achieve the best possible aesthetic outcome.

5. Are the results permanent?

FAQs about Gynecomastia Surgery in Jaipur

Curiosity is a natural companion on the path to transformation. As you consider gynecomastia surgery in Jaipur, it’s essential to have clear answers to your questions. Here, I address some of the most common inquiries to equip you with valuable insights:

1. What is gynecomastia, and who is an ideal candidate for the surgery?

Gynecomastia is a condition characterized by the enlargement of male breast tissue. It can be caused by hormonal imbalances, genetics, medications, or other factors. An ideal candidate for gynecomastia surgery is someone who:

  • Is in good overall health
  • Has realistic expectations about the outcomes
  • Experiences physical and emotional discomfort due to gynecomastia
  • Has reached a stable age when the chest is fully developed

2. How is the surgery performed?

Gynecomastia surgery involves the removal of excess glandular tissue and fat from the chest. The procedure can be performed through liposuction, excision, or a combination of both. Liposuction is used to remove excess fat, while excision is employed to address glandular tissue. Incisions are strategically placed to minimize visible scarring.

3. What is the recovery process like?

The recovery period varies from individual to individual. Initially, you may experience some discomfort, swelling, and bruising. However, these effects gradually subside. Most patients can resume light activities within a week. Strenuous exercises and heavy lifting should be avoided for a few weeks to promote proper healing.

4. What results can I expect from gynecomastia surgery?

Gynecomastia surgery can lead to a flatter, more masculine chest contour. The results are intended to be natural-looking and harmonious with your overall physique. With a healthy lifestyle and proper aftercare, the outcomes are generally long-lasting, providing you with enhanced self-confidence.

5. What is the cost of gynecomastia surgery in Jaipur?

The cost of gynecomastia surgery can vary based on factors such as the extent of the procedure, surgical techniques used, and facility fees. During your consultation, we will provide you with a personalized quote that reflects your unique treatment plan.
