Frequently Asked Questions about Gynecomastia Surgery in Jaipur

When considering a significant step like gynecomastia surgery, questions are natural. Here, I address five of the most commonly asked questions to provide you with insights and clarity on your journey towards male breast reduction in Jaipur.

Is gynecomastia surgery painful?

Gynecomastia surgery is typically performed under either general or local anesthesia, ensuring that you are comfortable throughout the procedure. Post-operative discomfort is normal, but any pain is usually manageable with prescribed pain medication. Rest assured that our team focuses on your comfort at every stage, and we provide detailed guidance on pain management during the recovery process.

How long does the recovery process usually take?

Recovery times can vary based on the extent of the procedure and individual factors. Generally, patients can expect some swelling and bruising in the days following surgery. Most individuals can resume light activities within a week and return to more strenuous activities after a few weeks. It’s important to follow post-operative care instructions closely to ensure a smooth recovery and optimal results.

What results can I realistically expect from the surgery?

Gynecomastia surgery can lead to a more contoured and balanced chest appearance. While results are often noticeable immediately after surgery, it’s important to note that final results may take a few months to fully emerge as swelling subsides and tissues settle. Our goal is to provide results that align with your expectations and enhance your self-confidence.

Are the results permanent?

In many cases, the results of gynecomastia surgery are permanent. However, maintaining a healthy lifestyle and avoiding significant weight fluctuations can contribute to the longevity of your results. During our consultations, I provide guidance on post-surgery care and factors that can influence the long-term outcome.

What qualifications make someone an ideal candidate for gynecomastia surgery?

Ideal candidates for gynecomastia surgery are individuals who are physically healthy, have realistic expectations, and are bothered by the appearance of enlarged breasts.
