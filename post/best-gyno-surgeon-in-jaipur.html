FAQs about Gynecomastia Surgery in Jaipur

Navigating the world of gynecomastia surgery can raise numerous questions, and seeking reliable information is crucial to making informed decisions. In this section, I’ll address five commonly asked questions about gynecomastia surgery to provide you with a comprehensive understanding of what to expect.

1. Is gynecomastia surgery painful?

Gynecomastia surgery is typically performed under anesthesia, ensuring that you won’t feel any pain during the procedure. Some discomfort and soreness are normal during the recovery phase, but this can be effectively managed with prescribed pain medications. Rest assured that my priority is to minimize any discomfort you may experience and guide you through a smooth recovery process.

3. Can gynecomastia return after surgery?

When performed by a skilled and experienced surgeon, gynecomastia surgery provides long-lasting results. However, maintaining a healthy lifestyle is essential to preventing a recurrence of the condition. Factors such as weight fluctuations, hormonal changes, and certain medications can influence the outcome. I provide guidance on post-operative care and lifestyle habits to help you maintain your results over the long term.

4. How long is the recovery period?

Recovery times can vary based on the extent of the surgery and individual healing rates. Most patients can return to non-strenuous activities within a week after gynecomastia surgery. However, it’s important to avoid vigorous exercise and heavy lifting for a few weeks to ensure proper healing. During follow-up appointments, I monitor your progress and provide guidance on resuming normal activities.

Absolutely. My goal as a skilled gynecomastia surgeon is to provide natural-looking results that are proportional to your body.
