FAQs about Gynecomastia Surgery in Jaipur

Embarking on a journey of transformation is a significant step, and it’s natural to have questions. In this section, I’ll address some of the most common queries about gynecomastia surgery, providing you with the information you need to make informed decisions.

1. What causes gynecomastia and who is prone to it?

Gynecomastia is primarily caused by hormonal imbalances, often involving an increase in estrogen levels compared to testosterone. While it can affect males of any age, it is more commonly observed during puberty, when hormonal fluctuations are more pronounced. Certain medical conditions, medications, and lifestyle factors can also contribute to its development.

2. Is gynecomastia surgery the only solution, or are there alternatives?

While lifestyle changes and targeted exercise can help reduce chest fat to some extent, they might not be effective in cases of true gynecomastia where glandular tissue is enlarged. In such cases, surgery is often the most effective solution. During consultations, I assess the nature of the condition and recommend the most suitable approach, which could include liposuction, glandular tissue removal, or a combination of both.

3. What does the recovery phase entail, and when can I resume regular activities?

Recovery after gynecomastia surgery varies from person to person. Generally, patients can expect some discomfort, swelling, and bruising in the initial weeks. A compression garment is typically worn to aid healing. Most individuals can resume light activities within a week and return to more strenuous activities within a few weeks, with the timeline varying based on the extent of the procedure.

4. Will there be noticeable scarring after surgery?

While scarring is an inherent part of any surgical procedure, I prioritize techniques that minimize scarring. Incisions are strategically placed in inconspicuous locations, and over time, scars tend to fade. Additionally, proper wound care during the recovery phase can contribute to optimal scar healing.

5. What long-term results can I expect from the procedure?

Gynecomastia surgery can bring about long-lasting results, provided that a healthy lifestyle is maintained. While the effects of aging, weight fluctuations, and hormonal changes might impact the chest’s appearance, the reduction in glandular tissue and fat achieved through surgery remains noticeable and significant.
