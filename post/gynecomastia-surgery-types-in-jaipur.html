1. What is the recovery period after gynecomastia surgery?

Recovery times can vary based on individual factors and the extent of the procedure. Typically, patients can expect to resume light activities within a week. Yes, the results of gynecomastia surgery are generally permanent. The excess glandular tissue and fat removed during the procedure do not grow back. However, it’s important to maintain a healthy lifestyle, including regular exercise and a balanced diet, to ensure long-lasting outcomes.

Modern surgical techniques, along with advanced pain management methods, have significantly minimized discomfort associated with gynecomastia surgery.
