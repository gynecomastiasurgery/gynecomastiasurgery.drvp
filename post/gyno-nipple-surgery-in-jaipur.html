Causes and Types

Gynecomastia can stem from various factors. Hormonal imbalances, certain medications, and even genetics can contribute to the development of this condition. There are two primary types of gynecomastia:

  1. True Gynecomastia: This type involves the growth of glandular breast tissue, resulting in a firmer enlargement.
  2. Pseudo-Gynecomastia: Here, the enlargement is primarily due to excess fat accumulation, resulting in a softer appearance.
