FAQs: Answers to Commonly Asked Questions about Gynecomastia Surgery in Jaipur

In this section, we address some frequently asked questions about gynecomastia surgery, shedding light on important aspects of the procedure, recovery, and expectations.

Q1: What causes gynecomastia?

Gynecomastia can be caused by a variety of factors, including hormonal imbalances, genetics, certain medications, and even medical conditions. It’s essential to undergo a thorough evaluation by a qualified plastic surgeon to determine the underlying cause and formulate an appropriate treatment plan.

Q2: How long is the recovery period after surgery?

The recovery period can vary from person to person, but most patients can typically resume normal activities within a few weeks after surgery. Strenuous activities and heavy lifting should be avoided for a few weeks to allow for proper healing.

Q3: Are the results of gynecomastia surgery permanent?

Yes, the results of gynecomastia surgery are usually long-lasting. However, maintaining a healthy lifestyle, including proper diet and exercise, is crucial to ensuring the longevity of the results.

Q4: Can gynecomastia surgery be performed using minimally invasive techniques?

In some cases, minimally invasive techniques may be suitable, depending on the severity of the condition and the patient’s individual needs. A thorough evaluation by a skilled surgeon will determine the most appropriate approach.

Q5: Are there any potential risks associated with gynecomastia surgery?

Like any surgical procedure, gynecomastia surgery carries certain risks, including infection, scarring, anesthesia-related complications, and unsatisfactory results. However, choosing an experienced surgeon and following pre- and post-operative instructions can significantly mitigate these risks.
