FAQ’s: Answering Your Common Questions

Navigating the world of gynecomastia surgery involves seeking clarity on various aspects of the procedure. Here, I address five commonly asked questions about gynecomastia surgery in Jaipur, providing you with insights to make informed decisions about your health and well-being:

Is gynecomastia surgery painful?

Gynecomastia surgery is performed under anesthesia, ensuring a comfortable experience during the procedure itself. You won’t feel any pain while the surgery is taking place. Post-surgery, some discomfort is natural, but it can be managed with prescribed pain medications. As you recover, any discomfort will gradually diminish, and you’ll be on your way to a transformed chest.

How long is the recovery period?

Recovery duration can vary depending on the extent of the procedure and individual healing capacity. Most patients can return to light activities within a week after surgery. However, it’s advised to avoid strenuous exercises and activities for about a month to ensure optimal healing and minimize potential complications. During the recovery period, following post-operative care instructions is crucial for the best outcomes.

Will there be visible scars?

Gynecomastia surgery involves strategically placed incisions to minimize visible scarring. While some scarring is inevitable, they are designed to be discreet and fade over time. Proper wound care and following aftercare instructions can contribute to the minimization of scarring. Rest assured, I prioritize aesthetic outcomes and take every effort to ensure your results are as natural-looking as possible.

Are the results permanent?

Gynecomastia surgery provides long-lasting results. Once the excess breast tissue and fat are removed, they do not typically return. However, maintaining a healthy lifestyle through regular exercise and a balanced diet can further enhance and sustain the results. It’s important to remember that individual results may vary, and I am committed to guiding you towards the most optimal outcomes.

Is the procedure safe?

Gynecomastia surgery is considered safe when performed by a qualified and experienced plastic surgeon.
