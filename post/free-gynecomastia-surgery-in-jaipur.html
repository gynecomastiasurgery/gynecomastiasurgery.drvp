FAQs: Commonly Asked Questions about Gynecomastia Surgery in Jaipur

1. Is gynecomastia surgery safe?

Absolutely. Gynecomastia surgery is generally safe when performed by a skilled and experienced plastic surgeon like myself. I prioritize patient safety and follow stringent medical protocols to minimize risks and ensure optimal outcomes. During your consultation, I will thoroughly evaluate your medical history and discuss any potential risks associated with the procedure.

2. Are the results of surgery permanent?

Yes, the results of gynecomastia surgery are generally long-lasting. However, it’s important to maintain a healthy lifestyle to ensure the best and most sustainable outcomes. This includes maintaining a stable weight, following a balanced diet, and engaging in regular exercise.

3. Is the procedure painful?

While some discomfort is to be expected after gynecomastia surgery, pain is typically manageable with prescribed pain medications. I will provide you with detailed post-operative care instructions to help you manage any discomfort and promote a smooth recovery process.

4. Will there be visible scars after surgery?

I understand the concern about scarring. I utilize advanced surgical techniques to minimize scarring as much as possible. Incisions are strategically placed in inconspicuous areas, such as along the natural contours of the chest. Over time, these scars tend to fade and become less noticeable.

5. Can non-surgical treatments completely eliminate gynecomastia?

Non-surgical treatments can significantly reduce breast tissue and improve chest contours, especially for individuals with mild to moderate gynecomastia. However, the extent of reduction achieved may vary from person to person. For more severe cases, surgical options might be more effective in achieving the desired results.
