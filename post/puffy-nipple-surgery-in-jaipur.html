FAQs: Gynecomastia Surgery in Jaipur

Navigating the realm of gynecomastia surgery involves questions and concerns. Here, I address some of the most commonly asked questions to provide you with a clearer understanding of the process and its outcomes.

1. What causes gynecomastia?

Gynecomastia can stem from hormonal imbalances, certain medications, or underlying health conditions. It’s important to undergo a comprehensive evaluation to determine the underlying cause in each case.

2. Is the surgery painful?

Gynecomastia surgery is performed under anesthesia, ensuring a painless experience during the procedure. Post-operatively, some discomfort is normal, but it’s manageable with prescribed pain medication. Our team provides comprehensive aftercare instructions to ease your recovery.

3. How long is the recovery period?

Recovery duration varies from person to person, but most patients can resume non-strenuous activities within about a week after surgery. Strenuous physical activities can typically be resumed gradually after a few weeks, as guided by your surgeon.

4. Are the results permanent?

Yes, the results of gynecomastia surgery are typically permanent. However, maintaining a healthy lifestyle is crucial to ensure lasting outcomes. Significant weight fluctuations or hormonal imbalances in the future could potentially affect the results.

5. Are there any risks involved?

Like any surgical procedure, gynecomastia surgery carries inherent risks. These include potential complications such as infection, bleeding, scarring, or changes in nipple sensation. However, with a skilled surgeon and adherence to post-operative care instructions, these risks are minimized.
