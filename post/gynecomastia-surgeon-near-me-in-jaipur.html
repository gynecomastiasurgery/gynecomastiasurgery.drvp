As you embark on the journey towards gynecomastia surgery in Jaipur, it’s natural to have questions and concerns. Here, we address some of the most frequently asked questions to provide you with clarity and confidence as you consider this transformative step.

1. Is gynecomastia surgery painful?

Gynecomastia surgery is performed under anesthesia, ensuring your comfort during the procedure itself. After surgery, you may experience some discomfort, which is manageable with prescribed pain medication. It’s important to note that post-surgery pain is temporary and typically subsides within a few days as you heal.

2. Will there be visible scars after surgery?

Dr. Vishal Purohit employs advanced techniques to minimize scarring. Incisions are strategically placed in discreet areas to ensure that scars are as inconspicuous as possible. Over time, these scars often fade and become barely noticeable, allowing you to enjoy your results without any visible signs of surgery.

3. How long is the recovery period?

Recovery time varies from person to person, but most patients can expect to resume light activities within a few days after surgery. Strenuous physical activities should be avoided for a few weeks to allow your body to heal properly. Dr. Purohit and his team provide you with clear post-operative instructions to ensure a smooth and efficient recovery.

4. Are the results permanent?

Yes, the results of gynecomastia surgery are long-lasting. The excess breast tissue and fat that are removed during the procedure do not grow back. However, it’s important to maintain a healthy lifestyle to ensure the longevity of your results. Significant weight fluctuations could potentially impact the outcome, so it’s advised to maintain a stable weight.

5. Is gynecomastia surgery covered by insurance?

In many cases, gynecomastia surgery is considered a cosmetic procedure and is not covered by insurance. However, Dr. Purohit’s team can provide guidance on financing options that can make the procedure more affordable.
