1. Is gynecomastia surgery safe?

2. What is the recovery process like?

Recovery time after gynecomastia surgery can vary from person to person, but most patients can resume non-strenuous activities within about a week. It’s advisable to avoid more strenuous activities for a few weeks to allow your body to heal optimally. Wearing a compression garment is typically recommended to support the healing process and minimize swelling.

3. Will there be visible scars?

While some scarring is inevitable with any surgical procedure, we take great care to minimize visible scarring. Our skilled surgeons strategically place incisions in areas that are less noticeable. Additionally, we provide post-operative care instructions to promote proper healing and scar management.

4. Can gynecomastia recur after surgery?

In most cases, the results of gynecomastia surgery are long-lasting and can be considered permanent. However, it’s important to address any underlying hormonal imbalances or factors that contributed to the initial development of gynecomastia. Our comprehensive approach aims to address these factors to reduce the likelihood of recurrence.

5. Are the results of the surgery natural-looking?

Yes, absolutely. Our surgical techniques are designed to create natural-looking results that blend seamlessly with your chest’s contours. We strive to achieve a balanced and masculine chest appearance that aligns with your body’s proportions.
