FAQs: Gynecomastia Surgery in Jaipur

Frequently Asked Questions about Gynecomastia Surgery

As a gynecomastia specialist in Jaipur, I’m here to address some common questions you might have about gyno plastic surgery. Let’s dive into these FAQs to provide you with a clearer understanding of what to expect:

1. What causes gynecomastia?

Gyno, or gynecomastia, results from an imbalance between estrogen and testosterone hormones. This imbalance can be triggered by factors such as puberty, hormonal changes, certain medications, and underlying medical conditions. Understanding the underlying cause is essential in tailoring the appropriate treatment plan.

2. Is surgery the only solution for gyno removal?

While exercise and lifestyle changes can help in mild cases, surgery is often the most effective solution for gyno removal, especially when excess breast tissue is present. Gynecomastia surgery ensures comprehensive removal of the tissue and provides more lasting results compared to non-surgical methods.

3. What is the surgical process like?

Gynecomastia surgery involves making small incisions in inconspicuous areas to remove excess breast tissue. The procedure is typically performed under anesthesia, and advanced techniques are used to minimize scarring. The incisions are strategically placed to ensure discreet healing.

4. What can I expect during the recovery period?

After surgery, you’ll wear a compression garment to aid healing and reduce swelling. Most patients can return to non-strenuous activities within a week or two. It’s important to follow post-operative care instructions, attend follow-up appointments, and be patient with the healing process.

5. Will there be noticeable scars after surgery?

Our surgical techniques are designed to minimize scarring. Incisions are placed in areas where scars are less likely to be visible. While scars are a natural part of any surgical procedure, they often fade over time and become less noticeable.

6. Are the results of gyno surgery permanent?

Barring significant weight gain or hormonal changes, the results of gynecomastia surgery are generally permanent. It’s crucial to maintain a healthy lifestyle to preserve your results and ensure long-term satisfaction.
