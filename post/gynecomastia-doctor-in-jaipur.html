FAQs about Gynecomastia Surgery in Jaipur

As a dedicated plastic surgeon specializing in gynecomastia correction in Jaipur, I understand that you might have questions about the procedure, recovery, and outcomes. Here, I address some of the most commonly asked questions to provide you with accurate and helpful information.

1. How long does the gynecomastia surgery procedure usually take?

The duration of the procedure can vary based on factors such as the extent of correction needed and the specific surgical techniques employed. On average, gynecomastia surgery takes approximately 1 to 2 hours. During your consultation, we’ll provide you with a more accurate estimate based on your unique case.

2. Will there be visible scars after the surgery?

I understand that scarring is a concern for many patients. That’s why I utilize advanced techniques to minimize scarring as much as possible. Incisions are strategically placed to ensure they are discreet and easily concealed. Over time, any scars that do form typically fade and become less noticeable.

3. When can I resume my normal activities after surgery?

Recovery timelines can vary, but most patients can return to light activities within about a week after surgery. It’s important to gradually increase your activity levels and avoid strenuous exercises for a few weeks. I’ll provide you with detailed post-operative instructions to guide you through a safe and effective recovery.

4. Is there a chance of gynecomastia recurring after surgery?

When gynecomastia surgery is performed by a skilled and experienced surgeon, the chances of recurrence are minimal. However, maintaining a healthy lifestyle, which includes regular exercise and a balanced diet, can further reduce the risk of recurrence.

5. Are the results of gynecomastia surgery permanent?

Yes, the results of gynecomastia surgery are generally permanent. The excess breast tissue and fat are removed during the procedure, leading to a more masculine chest contour. However, it’s important to maintain a healthy lifestyle to enjoy lasting results.
