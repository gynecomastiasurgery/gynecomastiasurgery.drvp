FAQs: Commonly Asked Questions About Gynecomastia Surgery in Jaipur

Navigating the world of Gynecomastia surgery can raise a multitude of questions. We’re here to provide clarity and address your concerns. Here are answers to some of the most frequently asked questions:

Is Gynecomastia surgery safe?

Absolutely. Your safety is our top priority. We utilize advanced surgical techniques and adhere to strict safety protocols to ensure a smooth procedure and optimal outcomes. During your consultation, we’ll discuss the process in detail, alleviating any worries you may have.

What is the recovery period after surgery?

The recovery period varies depending on the extent of the procedure. Most patients can return to light activities within a week. Over the next few weeks, you can gradually increase your activity level. Following post-operative instructions diligently will promote a quicker and smoother recovery.

Will there be visible scars?

Our surgical techniques are designed to minimize scarring. Incisions are strategically placed in inconspicuous areas whenever possible. While some scars may initially appear, they typically fade significantly over time, becoming less noticeable as you heal.

Can Gynecomastia recur after surgery?

In the majority of cases, the results of Gynecomastia surgery are permanent. However, maintaining a healthy lifestyle is important to prevent any potential recurrence. This includes maintaining a stable weight and avoiding factors that could contribute to hormonal imbalances.

How long before I see the final results?

While you’ll notice initial results once swelling subsides (usually a few weeks post-surgery), the final outcome becomes more apparent over the next few months. During this time, your body will continue to heal and adjust, revealing the transformed chest contour you’ve been striving for.
